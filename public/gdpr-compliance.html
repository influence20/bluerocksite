GDPR Compliance

How BlueRock Asset Management protects your personal data and respects your privacy rights

Our Commitment to Data Protection

At BlueRock Asset Management, we are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy rights. We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), which is considered the gold standard for data protection worldwide.

The GDPR gives individuals greater control over their personal data and places obligations on organizations that collect and process this data. As a financial services provider, we take these obligations seriously and have implemented comprehensive measures to ensure compliance.

Lawfulness

We only process data with a valid legal basis.

Purpose Limitation

We collect data for specific, explicit purposes.

Data Minimization

We collect only what's necessary for our purposes.

Accuracy

We maintain accurate and up-to-date data.

Storage Limitation

We don't keep data longer than needed.

Security

We protect data with appropriate measures.

Your Rights Under GDPR

The GDPR provides you with several important rights regarding your personal data. At BlueRock Asset Management, we respect and facilitate these rights:

Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

You can request that we correct any inaccurate data we hold about you.

Right to Erasure

You can request that we delete your personal data (subject to certain conditions).

Right to Restriction

You can request that we temporarily or permanently stop processing your data.

Right to Portability

You can request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.

Right to Object

You can object to our processing of your data for certain purposes.

Rights Related to Automated Decision Making

You have rights regarding automated decisions with significant effects.

Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.

Data Security Measures

Protecting your personal data is our priority. We have implemented comprehensive technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of your data:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols.
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls ensure that only authorized personnel can access personal data on a need-to-know basis.
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to identify and address potential risks.
  • Staff Training: All staff members receive regular training on data protection and security best practices.
  • Incident Response Plan: We have a comprehensive incident response plan to quickly address any potential data breaches.
  • Secure Infrastructure: Our IT infrastructure is designed with security in mind, including firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and regular security updates.
  • Third-Party Assessments: We regularly assess the security measures of our third-party service providers who may process personal data on our behalf.
  • Data Minimization: We collect and retain only the personal data that is necessary for our specified purposes.

In the unlikely event of a data breach that may pose a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify both the relevant supervisory authority and affected individuals in accordance with GDPR requirements.

International Data Transfers

As a global financial services provider, we may transfer your personal data to countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). When we do so, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs): We use EU-approved Standard Contractual Clauses in our agreements with third parties who process data outside the EEA.
  • Adequacy Decisions: Where possible, we transfer data to countries that have been deemed to provide an adequate level of protection by the European Commission.
  • Privacy Shield: For transfers to the United States, we work with organizations that are certified under the EU-US Privacy Shield Framework where applicable.
  • Binding Corporate Rules: Within our corporate group, we may rely on Binding Corporate Rules approved by EU data protection authorities.

We conduct regular assessments of countries where your data may be processed to ensure they provide adequate protection for your rights and freedoms.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Our retention periods are determined based on:

  • The amount, nature, and sensitivity of the personal data
  • The potential risk of harm from unauthorized use or disclosure
  • The purposes for which we process the data
  • Whether we can achieve those purposes through other means
  • Legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements

Typical retention periods for different types of data include:

Data Category Retention Period Reason
Account Information 7 years after account closure Financial regulations, tax requirements
Transaction Data 10 years Financial regulations, audit requirements
KYC Documentation 5 years after business relationship ends Anti-money laundering regulations
Marketing Preferences Until consent is withdrawn Based on consent
Website Logs 12 months Security and troubleshooting

Once the retention period expires, personal data is securely deleted or anonymized in accordance with our data deletion procedures.

Cookie Management

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. In compliance with GDPR, we provide you with control over the cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in, or filling in forms.
  • Performance Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. They help us improve the way our website works.
  • Functional Cookies: These enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.
  • Targeting Cookies: These may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on other sites.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking on the "Cookie Settings" button in the footer of our website. You can also clear cookies and change your settings through your browser settings.

For more detailed information about the cookies we use, please visit our Cookie Policy.
