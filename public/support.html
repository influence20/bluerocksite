Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

How long does it take to process withdrawals?

Withdrawal requests are typically processed within 24-48 hours. Once approved, the funds will be transferred to your specified cryptocurrency wallet. The actual time for the funds to appear in your wallet depends on the blockchain network's confirmation time.

What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum investment amount is $300 with our Starter Plan. This plan provides $180 in weekly returns for 3 months, totaling $2,160 in profits.

How do I get a withdrawal PIN?

Withdrawal PINs are generated by our admin team when you request a withdrawal. Once your withdrawal request is submitted, our team will review it and generate a secure PIN that will be sent to your registered email address. This PIN is required to complete the withdrawal process.

What cryptocurrencies do you accept for deposits?

We accept deposits in Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Tether (USDT), and BNB. Each cryptocurrency has its own dedicated wallet address for deposits. Make sure to send the correct cryptocurrency to its corresponding address.

How do I track my investment performance?

You can track your investment performance through your client dashboard. The dashboard provides real-time updates on your investment balance, weekly returns, and total profits. You can also view your transaction history and download statements for your records.
