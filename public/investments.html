How Our Premium Investment Program Works

Our Premium Investment Program offers an exceptional opportunity to earn consistent weekly returns over a 3-month period. Here's how it works:

  1. Choose Your Investment Amount: Start with as little as $300. Higher investments yield proportionally higher returns.
  2. Make Your Deposit: Deposit your chosen amount using cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, BNB, or USDT).
  3. Receive Weekly Returns: Every Friday, you'll receive your weekly returns directly to your account.
  4. Track Your Progress: Monitor your investment performance through your personalized dashboard.
  5. Complete Investment Term: After 3 months (12 weeks), your investment term is complete with a total ROI of 720%.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment amount?

Our Premium Investment Program starts at just $300. This is our minimum investment amount across all our investment plans.

How are my investments managed?

At BlueRock Asset Management, your investments are managed by a team of experienced financial professionals using a combination of active and passive strategies. We create diversified portfolios based on your risk tolerance, financial goals, and time horizon. Our investment committee regularly reviews and adjusts portfolios to respond to market conditions while maintaining alignment with your long-term objectives.

How often will I receive returns from the Premium Investment Program?

With our Premium Investment Program, you'll receive weekly returns every Friday for the entire 3-month (12-week) period. These returns are automatically credited to your account and can be withdrawn at any time.

Can I withdraw my investment before the end of the term?

For our Premium Investment Program, your principal investment is locked for the 3-month period to generate the promised returns. However, you can withdraw your weekly returns at any time. For other investment portfolios, withdrawal terms vary based on the specific product, and early withdrawal fees may apply.

How do I track my investment performance?

All clients have access to our secure online dashboard where you can monitor your investment performance in real-time. The dashboard provides detailed information about your portfolio value, returns, transaction history, and upcoming payments. For additional support, our client service team is available to answer any questions about your investments.

What cryptocurrencies do you accept for deposits?

We currently accept Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Binance Coin (BNB), and Tether (USDT) for deposits. All cryptocurrency values are converted to USD at the time of deposit based on current market rates.
