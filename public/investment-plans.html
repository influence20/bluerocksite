Frequently Asked Questions

How do the investment plans work?

Our investment plans are designed to provide consistent weekly returns over a 3-month period. You make a one-time investment, and we pay you a fixed amount every Friday for 12 weeks. The weekly return is proportional to your investment amount, with all plans offering a 720% ROI over the investment period.

What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum investment amount is $300 with our Starter Plan. This plan provides $180 in weekly returns for 3 months, totaling $2,160 in profits.

When do I receive my weekly returns?

Weekly returns are paid every Friday directly to your account. You can track all payments in your client dashboard and request withdrawals at any time.

Can I withdraw my initial investment before the 3-month period ends?

Your initial investment is locked for the 3-month period to generate the promised returns. However, you can withdraw your weekly returns at any time without affecting your investment plan.

How do I make a deposit?

We accept deposits in various cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), BNB, and Tether (USDT). After creating your account, navigate to the Deposit page where you'll find our wallet addresses for each cryptocurrency.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

You can request withdrawals through your client dashboard. Our admin team will generate a secure withdrawal PIN that you'll need to confirm your withdrawal request. Once confirmed, withdrawals are processed within 24 hours.
